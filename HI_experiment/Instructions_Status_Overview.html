EXAMPLE SCREEN

At the beginning of each round, you will be presented with the "Status Overview" Page.

While you are in this page, you can accumulate extra points by answering trivia questions correctly. To do so, select your answer and press the gray "Next" button.

You can also see some of your information on the right side of the screen:

  • Finances: Shows your current balance and fixed income.
  • Health insurance: Displays the Premium and Deductible for your current contract.
  • Risk Profile: Shows the probability of different health costs that can take place, as well as their cost.

You have {{ Constants.timeout }} minutes per round in the "Status overview" page, if you wish to move to the next page, you can click the blue "Next" button.
Remember, minor and major health events are not mutually exclusive. Minor health events affect one round and major health events affect 2 rounds.
